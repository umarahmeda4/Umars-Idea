❓ Help & Feature Guide
Everything you need to know about Umar's Idea
⊟ Dashboard
Your daily overview. Shows today's appointments, pending labs, unread messages, and active patient count. Critical Alerts appear here for patients needing attention. Click any stat card to jump straight to that section.
👤 Patients
+ New Patient — add a patient manually with name, DOB, blood type, allergies and medications.
📋 Intake Form — fill in with the patient at reception. Creates the full record automatically.
View EHR — opens the patient detail panel with their full record.
⚠️ Red allergy banner — appears automatically when a patient with allergies is opened.
📊 Vitals — log BP, heart rate, SpO₂, temperature, weight, height. Abnormal values flagged in red.
🧪 Labs — log a 10-panel blood test. Abnormal results flagged automatically.
💊 Rx — open the Prescription Writer for that patient. Add multiple drugs, then print or save.
🖨 Print — prints a full patient summary with your clinic letterhead.
+ Note — add a clinical note (SOAP, follow-up, procedure, referral, etc.).
🎤 Dictate — speak a note using your microphone.
📅 Schedule
Calendar view — week or month. Navigate with ← → arrows. Click any slot to book an appointment.
Waiting Room — shows today's appointments with live status. Click a row to update status.
Appointment statuses: Scheduled → Checked In → In Progress → Completed / No-Show.
Conflict warning — alerts you if you try to double-book the same time slot.
📹 Telemedicine
Launch virtual consultations directly from the app. Session notes can be saved to the patient record after the call.
🔬 Clinical Tools
Drug lookup — search for medications, dosing, interactions.
ICD-10 codes — search and attach diagnosis codes to records.
Clinical guidelines — quick reference for common conditions.
Risk calculators — BMI, eGFR, cardiovascular risk, and more.
💬 Messaging
Secure internal messaging between accounts on the same device. New Message lets you search for other registered accounts. Messages sync every 5 seconds. Conversations show with blue (you) and grey (them) chat bubbles.
🧾 Billing
Log claims and track revenue. + New Claim attaches a billing code and amount to a patient visit. The analytics page shows revenue breakdowns using this data. Billing target is set in Settings.
📊 Analytics
Visual breakdowns of your practice. Filter by Week / Month / Year / All. Shows patient volume, revenue, diagnosis frequency, and appointment trends.
🎓 CME & Education
Browse continuing medical education courses. Claim CME credits by completing courses — each is marked with a green ✓ once claimed so you can't double-count.
🔐 Security & Settings
Account Settings — set your display name, specialty, clinic/hospital name, and monthly billing target. Your clinic name and doctor name appear on all printed documents.
Audit Log — every action (sign-in, record access, edit, prescription) is logged here.
Role-Based Access — add other staff (nurses, receptionists) with different permission levels.
🖨️ Printing & Letterhead
All printouts (patient summaries, prescriptions) include your clinic letterhead automatically. To set it up go to Security → Account Settings and fill in your Display Name, Specialty, and Hospital/Practice name.
⌨️ Tips & Shortcuts
Search bar (top) — search patients by name, MRN, or condition from anywhere in the app.
✏️ Quick Note (top right) — jot a fast note without opening a patient record.
🌙 Dark mode (top right) — toggle dark/light theme. Your preference is saved.
🔔 Notifications — see recent alerts. Click "Clear all" to dismiss them.
Keep me signed in — if checked at login, you'll be taken straight to the app next time you open the file.
Password — the first password you use to sign in becomes your permanent password for that account.
